FPS HUD
Overview

FPS HUD is a lightweight Minecraft mod that displays your real-time FPS on-screen. Itâ€™s fully customizable, allowing you to toggle the HUD, adjust its position, and scale it to fit your screen. Perfect for monitoring performance while keeping your gameplay clean and minimalistic.

Features

Real-time FPS display

Customizable position and scale of the HUD

Toggleable display with a default keybind (K)

Lightweight and optimized for performance

Minimalistic, clean design for smooth gameplay

Installation

Download the FPS HUD .jar file.

Place it in your Minecraft mods folder.

Launch Minecraft with Fabric Loader.

Press K to toggle the HUD on or off, or configure settings in the menu.
